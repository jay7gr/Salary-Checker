Key Insights

The gap between the most and least affordable neighborhoods is staggering. Your purchasing power can differ by 10x or more depending on where you choose to live. Even within the same city, choosing the right neighborhood can effectively give you a 30-50% raise.

Remote workers in particular can leverage this data: earning a Western salary while living in a high-purchasing-power neighborhood lets you build wealth dramatically faster.

Methodology

Purchasing power is calculated as: (100 / Absolute COLI) x 100, where Absolute COLI = City COLI x Neighborhood Multiplier. Data sourced from Numbeo, Expatistan, OECD, and ECB, combined with local rental and consumer surveys.

About Austin

Austin has a cost of living index of 64.8, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #29 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is USD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately $48,600 in Austin to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in United States is 12% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 19.5%.

About Manila

Manila has a cost of living index of 26.8, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #78 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is PHP. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ₱1,127,598 in Manila to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Philippines is 16% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 21.4%.

Data Sources & Methodology

Our cost of living index (COLI) uses New York City as the baseline (COLI = 100). Indices are derived from multiple sources including Numbeo, Expatistan, and national statistics agencies. Exchange rates are sourced from the European Central Bank.

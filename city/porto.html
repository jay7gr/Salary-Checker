About Porto

Porto has a cost of living index of 30.1, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #73 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €19,141 in Porto to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Portugal is 18% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 29.5%.

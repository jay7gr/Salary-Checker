About Málaga

Málaga has a cost of living index of 31.2, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #79 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €19,841 in Málaga to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Spain is 21% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 27.2%.

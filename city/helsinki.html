About Helsinki

Helsinki has a cost of living index of 58.2, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #38 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €37,010 in Helsinki to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Finland is 31% for a mid-range earner.

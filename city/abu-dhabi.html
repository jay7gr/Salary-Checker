About Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi has a cost of living index of 67.8, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #26 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in UAE is 0%. This is a tax-free jurisdiction.

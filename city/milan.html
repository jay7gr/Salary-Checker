About Milan

Milan has a cost of living index of 57.8, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #39 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €36,756 in Milan to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Italy is 27% for a mid-range earner.

Data Sources & Methodology

Our cost of living index (COLI) uses New York City as the baseline (COLI = 100). Indices are derived from multiple sources including Numbeo, Expatistan, and national statistics agencies. Exchange rates are sourced from the European Central Bank.

Tax rates represent approximate effective rates for mid-range earners based on OECD Taxing Wages data and do not constitute tax advice.

About Cape Town

Cape Town has a cost of living index of 28.4, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #76 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is ZAR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately R 388,148 in Cape Town to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in South Africa is 26% for a mid-range earner.

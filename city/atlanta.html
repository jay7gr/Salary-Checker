About Atlanta

Atlanta has a cost of living index of 63.2, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #36 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is USD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately $47,400 in Atlanta to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in United States is 12% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 24.7%.

About Panama City

Panama City has a cost of living index of 42.3, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #64 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is USD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately $31,725 in Panama City to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Panama is 10% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 19.5%.

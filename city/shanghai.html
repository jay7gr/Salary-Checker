About Shanghai

Shanghai has a cost of living index of 46.2, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #48 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is CNY. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ¥233,261 in Shanghai to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in China is 20% for a mid-range earner.

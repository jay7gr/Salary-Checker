About Fukuoka

Fukuoka has a cost of living index of 51.4, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #42 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is JPY. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ¥5,971,767 in Fukuoka to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Japan is 13% for a mid-range earner.

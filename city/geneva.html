About Geneva

Geneva has a cost of living index of 118.6, making it a very high cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #2 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is CHF. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately CHF 72,071 in Geneva to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Switzerland is 10% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 42.2%.

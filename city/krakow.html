About Krakow

Krakow has a cost of living index of 23.8, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #89 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is PLN. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately zł 69,895 in Krakow to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Poland is 17% for a mid-range earner.

About Amsterdam

Amsterdam has a cost of living index of 82.6, making it a high cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #8 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €52,527 in Amsterdam to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Netherlands is 37% for a mid-range earner.

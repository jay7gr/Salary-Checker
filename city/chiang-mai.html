About Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai has a cost of living index of 22.6, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #92 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is THB. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ฿525,310 in Chiang Mai to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Thailand is 15% for a mid-range earner.

Data Sources & Methodology

Our cost of living index (COLI) uses New York City as the baseline (COLI = 100). Indices are derived from multiple sources including Numbeo, Expatistan, and national statistics agencies. Exchange rates are sourced from the European Central Bank.

Tax rates represent approximate effective rates for mid-range earners based on OECD Taxing Wages data and do not constitute tax advice.

About Chiang Mai

Chiang Mai has a cost of living index of 22.6, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #92 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is THB. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ฿525,310 in Chiang Mai to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Thailand is 6% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 7.7%.

About Casablanca

Casablanca has a cost of living index of 22.1, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #95 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is MAD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately MAD 161,007 in Casablanca to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Morocco is 23% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 29.6%.

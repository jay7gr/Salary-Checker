About Taipei

Taipei has a cost of living index of 44.8, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #49 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is TWD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately NT$1,062,651 in Taipei to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Taiwan is 8% for a mid-range earner.

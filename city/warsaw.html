About Warsaw

Warsaw has a cost of living index of 28.4, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #75 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is PLN. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately zł 83,404 in Warsaw to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Poland is 8% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 21.4%.

About Jakarta

Jakarta has a cost of living index of 24.5, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #99 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is IDR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately Rp 283,650,226 in Jakarta to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Indonesia is 14% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 19.1%.

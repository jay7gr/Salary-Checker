About Montevideo

Montevideo has a cost of living index of 32.5, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #65 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is UYU. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately $U 1,024,191 in Montevideo to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Uruguay is 20% for a mid-range earner.

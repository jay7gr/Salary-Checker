About Nashville

Nashville has a cost of living index of 62.8, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #37 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is USD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately $47,100 in Nashville to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in United States is 12% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 19.2%.

Data Sources & Methodology

Tax rates represent approximate effective rates for mid-range earners based on OECD Taxing Wages data and do not constitute tax advice.

About Bucharest

Bucharest has a cost of living index of 22.5, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #105 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is RON. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately lei 71,414 in Bucharest to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Romania is 10% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 45.0%.

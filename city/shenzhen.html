About Shenzhen

Shenzhen has a cost of living index of 41.9, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #54 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is CNY. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ¥211,551 in Shenzhen to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in China is 12% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 34.5%.

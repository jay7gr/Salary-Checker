About Miami

Miami has a cost of living index of 80.1, making it a high cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #11 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is USD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately $60,075 in Miami to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in United States is 14% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 21.4%.

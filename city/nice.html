About Nice

Nice has a cost of living index of 52.7, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #40 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €33,513 in Nice to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in France is 10% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 32.0%.

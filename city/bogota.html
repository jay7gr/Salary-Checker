About Bogotá

Bogotá has a cost of living index of 22.9, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #103 out of 113 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is COP. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately COP 71,390,060 in Bogotá to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Colombia is 7% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 14.7%.

About Guangzhou

Guangzhou has a cost of living index of 38.7, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #56 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is CNY. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ¥195,394 in Guangzhou to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in China is 11% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 33.8%.

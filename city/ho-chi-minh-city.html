About Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City has a cost of living index of 26.4, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #79 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is VND. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately ₫495,000,000 in Ho Chi Minh City to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Vietnam is 15% for a mid-range earner.

Data Sources & Methodology

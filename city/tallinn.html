About Tallinn

Tallinn has a cost of living index of 28.9, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #74 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is EUR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately €18,378 in Tallinn to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Estonia is 20% for a mid-range earner.

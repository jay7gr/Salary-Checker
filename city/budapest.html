About Budapest

Budapest has a cost of living index of 26.1, making it a very low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #80 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is HUF. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately Ft 7,031,081 in Budapest to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Hungary is 15% for a mid-range earner.

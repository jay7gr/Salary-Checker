About Doha

Doha has a cost of living index of 69.5, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #22 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Qatar is 0%. This is a tax-free jurisdiction.

About Perth

Perth has a cost of living index of 63.5, making it a moderate cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #31 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is AUD. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately A$67,672 in Perth to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Australia is 16% for a mid-range earner.

Tax rates represent approximate effective rates for mid-range earners based on OECD Taxing Wages data and do not constitute tax advice.

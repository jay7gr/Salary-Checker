About Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur has a cost of living index of 32.8, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #63 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is MYR. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately RM 100,030 in Kuala Lumpur to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate average effective income tax rate in Malaysia is 16% for a mid-range earner.

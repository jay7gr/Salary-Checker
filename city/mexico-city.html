About Mexico City

Mexico City has a cost of living index of 32.5, making it a low cost city compared to New York (baseline = 100). It ranks #64 out of 101 cities tracked globally.

The local currency is MXN. A salary of $75,000 USD in New York would need to be approximately MX$412,980 in Mexico City to maintain the same purchasing power.

The approximate effective income tax rate in Mexico is 17% for a mid-range earner. Including social security and other mandatory deductions, the total deduction rate is approximately 19.5%.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average ux designer salary in 2026?

The mid-career ux designer base salary is $75,000 (New York baseline). Entry-level starts around $40,000 and senior roles can reach $140,000. These figures are adjusted for each city using local cost of living.

Which city pays ux designers the most?

After adjusting for cost of living, Zurich offers the highest ux designer salary at $91,800 (USD equivalent). See the full table above for all 113 cities.

How is the salary adjusted for each city?

We start with the New York baseline salary and scale it by each city's Cost of Living Index (COLI). For example, a city with COLI 50 means living costs are half of New York, so the adjusted salary is 50% of the baseline. Take-home percentage accounts for local income tax and social contributions.

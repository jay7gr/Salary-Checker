How We Calculate This

We start with actual monthly costs in Cancún: rent for a 1-bedroom apartment, groceries, utilities, transport, and healthcare. For the "comfortable" tier, we apply the 50/30/20 rule — your essentials should be 50% of take-home pay, leaving 30% for wants and 20% for savings.

We then reverse-calculate the gross (pre-tax) salary you'd need, using Mexico's progressive tax brackets and mandatory deductions. All figures are in MXN for 2026.

Frequently Asked Questions

What salary do you need to live in Cancún?

To live comfortably in Cancún in 2026, you need a gross annual salary of approximately MX$628,943. This covers rent, groceries, utilities, transport, healthcare, and leaves 30% for wants and 20% for savings (50/30/20 rule). The minimum to get by is MX$296,032.

What is the cost of living in Cancún?

Monthly rent for a 1-bedroom apartment in Cancún is approximately MX$10,166. Essential monthly expenses (groceries, utilities, transport, healthcare) add roughly MX$10,216. The effective tax rate is 22.2%.

Is MX$296,032 enough to live in Cancún?

MX$296,032 is the bare minimum salary to get by in Cancún — it covers rent and essential expenses with very little left over. For a comfortable lifestyle with savings, you'd want at least MX$628,943.

Key Takeaways: Nice vs Phoenix

Overall, Nice is approximately 11% cheaper than Phoenix based on our cost of living index. Nice has a COLI of 52.7 (ranked #52 of 113 cities), while Phoenix has a COLI of 58.9 (ranked #47).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Nice averages $1,100/month compared to $1,450/month in Phoenix. That makes Nice approximately 24% cheaper for rent alone.

Phoenix has lower total deductions (21.6%) compared to Nice (32.0%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Phoenix. When evaluating a relocation, remember that total deductions directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

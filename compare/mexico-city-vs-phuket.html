Key Takeaways: Mexico City vs Phuket

Overall, Phuket is approximately 6% cheaper than Mexico City based on our cost of living index. Mexico City has a COLI of 32.5 (ranked #64 of 101 cities), while Phuket has a COLI of 30.5 (ranked #72).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Mexico City averages $700/month compared to $500/month in Phuket. That makes Phuket approximately 29% cheaper for rent alone.

Phuket has a lower effective tax rate (8%) compared to Mexico City (17%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Phuket. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

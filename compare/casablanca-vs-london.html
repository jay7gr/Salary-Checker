Key Takeaways: Casablanca vs London

Overall, Casablanca is approximately 296% cheaper than London based on our cost of living index. Casablanca has a COLI of 22.1 (ranked #95 of 101 cities), while London has a COLI of 87.5 (ranked #6).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Casablanca averages $400/month compared to $2,500/month in London. That makes Casablanca approximately 525% cheaper for rent alone.

London has a lower effective tax rate (15%) compared to Casablanca (23%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in London. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn MAD 728,539 in Casablanca, you would need approximately £223,604 in London to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 296% more due to higher cost of living.

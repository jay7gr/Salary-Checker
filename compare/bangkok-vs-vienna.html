Key Takeaways: Bangkok vs Vienna

Overall, Bangkok is approximately 30% cheaper than Vienna based on our cost of living index. Bangkok has a COLI of 37.2 (ranked #58 of 101 cities), while Vienna has a COLI of 48.2 (ranked #46).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Bangkok averages $600/month compared to $1,000/month in Vienna. That makes Bangkok approximately 67% cheaper for rent alone.

Bangkok has lower total deductions (11.2%) compared to Vienna (32.6%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Bangkok. When evaluating a relocation, remember that total deductions directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Key Takeaways: Fukuoka vs San José (CR)

Overall, San José (CR) is approximately 39% cheaper than Fukuoka based on our cost of living index. Fukuoka has a COLI of 51.4 (ranked #42 of 101 cities), while San José (CR) has a COLI of 31.2 (ranked #68).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Fukuoka averages $700/month compared to $550/month in San José (CR). That makes San José (CR) approximately 21% cheaper for rent alone.

San José (CR) has a lower effective tax rate (1%) compared to Fukuoka (13%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in San José (CR). When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

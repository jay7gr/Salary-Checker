Key Takeaways: Playa del Carmen vs Sydney

Overall, Playa del Carmen is approximately 142% cheaper than Sydney based on our cost of living index. Playa del Carmen has a COLI of 30.8 (ranked #69 of 101 cities), while Sydney has a COLI of 74.5 (ranked #17).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Playa del Carmen averages $500/month compared to $2,200/month in Sydney. That makes Playa del Carmen approximately 340% cheaper for rent alone.

Playa del Carmen has a lower effective tax rate (20%) compared to Sydney (27%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Playa del Carmen. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Key Takeaways: Berlin vs Munich

Overall, Berlin is approximately 20% cheaper than Munich based on our cost of living index. Berlin has a COLI of 51.2 (ranked #43 of 101 cities), while Munich has a COLI of 61.5 (ranked #35).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Berlin averages $1,200/month compared to $1,500/month in Munich. That makes Berlin approximately 25% cheaper for rent alone.

Berlin has lower total deductions (34.7%) compared to Munich (36.6%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Berlin. When evaluating a relocation, remember that total deductions directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

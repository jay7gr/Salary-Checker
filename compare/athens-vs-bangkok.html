Key Takeaways: Athens vs Bangkok

Overall, Athens is approximately 22% cheaper than Bangkok based on our cost of living index. Athens has a COLI of 30.6 (ranked #70 of 101 cities), while Bangkok has a COLI of 37.2 (ranked #58).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Athens averages $700/month compared to $600/month in Bangkok. That makes Bangkok approximately 14% cheaper for rent alone.

Bangkok has a lower effective tax rate (10%) compared to Athens (15%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Bangkok. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

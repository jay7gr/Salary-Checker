Key Takeaways: Bali (Denpasar) vs Bogotá

Overall, Bogotá is approximately 9% cheaper than Bali (Denpasar) based on our cost of living index. Bali (Denpasar) has a COLI of 25.3 (ranked #84 of 101 cities), while Bogotá has a COLI of 22.9 (ranked #91).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Bali (Denpasar) averages $450/month compared to $400/month in Bogotá. That makes Bogotá approximately 11% cheaper for rent alone.

Bogotá has lower total deductions (14.7%) compared to Bali (Denpasar) (19.4%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Bogotá. When evaluating a relocation, remember that total deductions directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

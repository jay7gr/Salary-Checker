Key Takeaways: Bali (Denpasar) vs Riyadh

Overall, Bali (Denpasar) is approximately 68% cheaper than Riyadh based on our cost of living index. Bali (Denpasar) has a COLI of 25.3 (ranked #84 of 101 cities), while Riyadh has a COLI of 42.6 (ranked #51).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Bali (Denpasar) averages $450/month compared to $800/month in Riyadh. That makes Bali (Denpasar) approximately 78% cheaper for rent alone.

Riyadh has a lower effective tax rate (0%) compared to Bali (Denpasar) (15%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Riyadh. Saudi Arabia is a tax-free jurisdiction, which is a significant financial advantage. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Key Takeaways: Delhi vs Denver

Overall, Delhi is approximately 237% cheaper than Denver based on our cost of living index. Delhi has a COLI of 19.5 (ranked #98 of 101 cities), while Denver has a COLI of 65.7 (ranked #27).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Delhi averages $350/month compared to $1,900/month in Denver. That makes Delhi approximately 443% cheaper for rent alone.

Delhi has a lower effective tax rate (20%) compared to Denver (24%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Delhi. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn ₹6,240,587 in Delhi, you would need approximately $252,692 in Denver to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 237% more due to higher cost of living.

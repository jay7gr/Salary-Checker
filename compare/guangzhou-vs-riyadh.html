Key Takeaways: Guangzhou vs Riyadh

Overall, Guangzhou is approximately 10% cheaper than Riyadh based on our cost of living index. Guangzhou has a COLI of 38.7 (ranked #56 of 101 cities), while Riyadh has a COLI of 42.6 (ranked #51).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Guangzhou averages $750/month compared to $800/month in Riyadh. That makes Guangzhou approximately 7% cheaper for rent alone.

Riyadh has a lower effective tax rate (0%) compared to Guangzhou (11%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Riyadh. Saudi Arabia is a tax-free jurisdiction, which is a significant financial advantage. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

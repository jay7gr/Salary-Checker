Key Takeaways: Montreal vs Málaga

Overall, Málaga is approximately 40% cheaper than Montreal based on our cost of living index. Montreal has a COLI of 52.1 (ranked #41 of 101 cities), while Málaga has a COLI of 31.2 (ranked #67).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Montreal averages $1,400/month compared to $800/month in Málaga. That makes Málaga approximately 43% cheaper for rent alone.

Málaga has lower total deductions (27.2%) compared to Montreal (36.8%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Málaga. When evaluating a relocation, remember that total deductions directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Key Takeaways: Lisbon vs Zurich

Overall, Lisbon is approximately 253% cheaper than Zurich based on our cost of living index. Lisbon has a COLI of 34.7 (ranked #60 of 101 cities), while Zurich has a COLI of 122.4 (ranked #1).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Lisbon averages $1,000/month compared to $2,800/month in Zurich. That makes Lisbon approximately 180% cheaper for rent alone.

Zurich has a lower effective tax rate (18%) compared to Lisbon (25%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Zurich. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn €63,592 in Lisbon, you would need approximately CHF 214,352 in Zurich to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 253% more due to higher cost of living.

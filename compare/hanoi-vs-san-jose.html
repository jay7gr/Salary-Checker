Key Takeaways: Hanoi vs San José (CR)

Overall, Hanoi is approximately 29% cheaper than San José (CR) based on our cost of living index. Hanoi has a COLI of 24.1 (ranked #88 of 101 cities), while San José (CR) has a COLI of 31.2 (ranked #68).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Hanoi averages $450/month compared to $550/month in San José (CR). That makes Hanoi approximately 22% cheaper for rent alone.

San José (CR) has lower total deductions (11.2%) compared to Hanoi (26.9%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in San José (CR). When evaluating a relocation, remember that total deductions directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

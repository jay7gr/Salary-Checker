Key Takeaways: Berlin vs Split

Overall, Split is approximately 38% cheaper than Berlin based on our cost of living index. Berlin has a COLI of 51.2 (ranked #43 of 101 cities), while Split has a COLI of 31.8 (ranked #66).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Berlin averages $1,200/month compared to $650/month in Split. That makes Split approximately 46% cheaper for rent alone.

Berlin has a lower effective tax rate (14%) compared to Split (20%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Berlin. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn €63,592 in Berlin, you would need approximately €39,497 in Split to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 38% less thanks to lower cost of living.

Key Takeaways: Nice vs Vancouver

Overall, Nice is approximately 24% cheaper than Vancouver based on our cost of living index. Nice has a COLI of 52.7 (ranked #40 of 101 cities), while Vancouver has a COLI of 65.4 (ranked #28).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Nice averages $1,100/month compared to $2,100/month in Vancouver. That makes Nice approximately 91% cheaper for rent alone.

Nice has a lower effective tax rate (10%) compared to Vancouver (16%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Nice. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn €63,592 in Nice, you would need approximately C$126,014 in Vancouver to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 24% more due to higher cost of living.

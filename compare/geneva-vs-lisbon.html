Key Takeaways: Geneva vs Lisbon

Overall, Lisbon is approximately 71% cheaper than Geneva based on our cost of living index. Geneva has a COLI of 118.6 (ranked #2 of 101 cities), while Lisbon has a COLI of 34.7 (ranked #60).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Geneva averages $2,600/month compared to $1,000/month in Lisbon. That makes Lisbon approximately 62% cheaper for rent alone.

Geneva has a lower effective tax rate (18%) compared to Lisbon (25%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Geneva. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn CHF 60,768 in Geneva, you would need approximately €18,606 in Lisbon to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 71% less thanks to lower cost of living.

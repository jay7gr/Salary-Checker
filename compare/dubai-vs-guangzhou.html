Key Takeaways: Dubai vs Guangzhou

Overall, Guangzhou is approximately 47% cheaper than Dubai based on our cost of living index. Dubai has a COLI of 72.4 (ranked #21 of 101 cities), while Guangzhou has a COLI of 38.7 (ranked #56).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Dubai averages $1,800/month compared to $750/month in Guangzhou. That makes Guangzhou approximately 58% cheaper for rent alone.

Dubai has a lower effective tax rate (0%) compared to Guangzhou (11%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Dubai. UAE is a tax-free jurisdiction, which is a significant financial advantage. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

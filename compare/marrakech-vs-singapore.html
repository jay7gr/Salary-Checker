Key Takeaways: Marrakech vs Singapore

Overall, Marrakech is approximately 357% cheaper than Singapore based on our cost of living index. Marrakech has a COLI of 19.2 (ranked #99 of 101 cities), while Singapore has a COLI of 87.7 (ranked #5).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Marrakech averages $350/month compared to $2,200/month in Singapore. That makes Marrakech approximately 529% cheaper for rent alone.

Singapore has a lower effective tax rate (5%) compared to Marrakech (22%), meaning you keep more of your gross salary in Singapore. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn MAD 728,539 in Marrakech, you would need approximately S$446,280 in Singapore to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 357% more due to higher cost of living.

Key Takeaways: Hong Kong vs Lima

Overall, Lima is approximately 65% cheaper than Hong Kong based on our cost of living index. Hong Kong has a COLI of 73.6 (ranked #18 of 101 cities), while Lima has a COLI of 25.8 (ranked #81).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Hong Kong averages $2,500/month compared to $450/month in Lima. That makes Lima approximately 82% cheaper for rent alone.

Both cities have the same effective tax rate of 15%. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn HK$587,349 in Hong Kong, you would need approximately S/97,403 in Lima to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 65% less thanks to lower cost of living.

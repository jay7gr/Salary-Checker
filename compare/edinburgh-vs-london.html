Key Takeaways: Edinburgh vs London

Overall, Edinburgh is approximately 41% cheaper than London based on our cost of living index. Edinburgh has a COLI of 62.1 (ranked #34 of 101 cities), while London has a COLI of 87.5 (ranked #6).

When it comes to housing, one-bedroom apartment rent in Edinburgh averages $1,300/month compared to $2,500/month in London. That makes Edinburgh approximately 92% cheaper for rent alone.

Both cities have the same effective tax rate of 25%. When evaluating a relocation, remember that tax rates directly impact your take-home pay and should be weighed alongside cost of living differences.

Salary Equivalent

If you earn £56,476 in Edinburgh, you would need approximately £79,576 in London to maintain the same purchasing power. That is 41% more due to higher cost of living.

Data Sources & Methodology

Our retirement data is compiled from multiple authoritative sources, cross-referenced for accuracy:

  • Climate: Weatherbase, Climate-Data.org

  • Tax Data: OECD Tax Database, Tax Foundation, national tax authority websites

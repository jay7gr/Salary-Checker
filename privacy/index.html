Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

salary-converter.com is a free salary comparison tool that helps users understand how their salary translates between cities, adjusted for cost of living, taxes, and currency differences. We are committed to transparency about how we handle your data.

What Data We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our salary converter, the cities and salary figures you enter are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or have access to the salary information you enter into the tool.

Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level)
  • Referral source (how you found our site)

Analytics data is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to personally identify you.

Cookies and Consent

Essential Cookies

We use a consent preference cookie (sc_consent) to remember your cookie choices. This cookie is set only if you interact with our consent banner and expires after 365 days.

Analytics

Google Analytics cookies are used on all pages to help us understand site usage patterns. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information. Analytics runs regardless of your consent preferences because it is essential to maintaining and improving our service.

Advertising Cookies

If you are visiting from a region where consent is required (EU/EEA countries, UK, Switzerland, Brazil, or Canada), we will show you a consent banner before any advertising cookies are set. You may:

  • Accept all cookies, including advertising
  • Choose More Options to toggle advertising cookies on or off individually
  • Reject All advertising cookies

If you are not in a strict consent region, advertising cookies may be set by default to support our ability to offer this tool for free.

Consent Regions

We show the consent banner to visitors from the following regions, in compliance with local data protection laws (GDPR, UK GDPR, LGPD, PIPEDA):

  • EU/EEA: Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway
  • Other: United Kingdom, Switzerland, Brazil, Canada

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 (analytics) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads (advertising, where consented) — Google Ads Privacy
  • Cloudflare (hosting and CDN) — Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • api.country.is (anonymous IP-to-country lookup for consent region detection)

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. We do not maintain any separate user database.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent — you can clear the sc_consent cookie from your browser at any time to reset your preferences
  • Right to object — opt out of advertising cookies via the consent banner

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, most data requests can be addressed by clearing your browser cookies.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please reach out via the contact options on our site.

# Daily Salary Converter Promotion — Shortcut Instructions

## Objective
Promote salary-converter.com daily on X (@SalaryConverter) and Quora (Jason Iliou) by engaging with relevant conversations and posting original content. Goal: build backlinks, authority, and organic traffic.

## About the Product
- **URL**: salary-converter.com
- **Key pages**: salary-converter.com/city/london, salary-converter.com/retire, salary-converter.com/blog
- **What it does**: Compare salaries across 182 cities and 3,400+ neighborhoods. Covers cost of living, taxes, rent, childcare. Free tool. Single & family mode. Updated for 2026.
- **Differentiator**: Neighborhood-level granularity (3,400+ neighborhoods), not just city-level.

## Daily Workflow

### Step 1: X — Find Relevant Conversations
Search X (logged in as @SalaryConverter) for recent posts using these queries:
- `"salary needed" OR "how much to earn" OR "equivalent salary" relocating`
- `"cost of living" salary city compare`
- `expat salary cost of living`
- `moving abroad salary equivalent`
- `what salary need London OR Dubai OR NYC OR Berlin OR Lisbon`
- `retire abroad cost of living budget`
- `remote work salary adjustment city`

Pick 1-2 posts that are recent (last 24-48h), have decent engagement (100+ views), and where salary-converter.com would genuinely help the person.

### Step 2: X — Post Replies
Reply to the selected posts with helpful, conversational replies. Rules:
- Lead with genuine value or insight, not the product
- Mention salary-converter.com naturally (not as the first thing you say)
- Keep it casual, not salesy
- Reference specific features (182 cities, 3,400+ neighborhoods, taxes, rent, childcare)
- Max 2 replies per day (new account has rate limits)

### Step 3: X — Post 1 Original Tweet
Rotate between these themes:
1. **Salary comparison awareness**: "Thinking about relocating? Don't just compare salaries — compare what you'll keep..."
2. **Retire abroad**: "Retiring abroad could stretch your pension 2-3x further..."
3. **City comparison hook**: "London vs Dubai vs Lisbon — same lifestyle, wildly different costs..."
4. **Remote work angle**: "Your company adjusts salary by location? Here's what the numbers actually look like..."
5. **Neighborhood insight**: "The difference between neighborhoods in the same city can be bigger than the difference between cities..."

Always include: salary-converter.com link + 3-5 relevant hashtags from: #relocation #expat #remotework #costofliving #salary #retireabroad #retirement #FIRE #digitalnomad #expatlife #movingabroad

### Step 4: Quora — Find & Answer Questions
Search Quora for unanswered or under-answered questions like:
- "What salary do I need to live comfortably in [city]?"
- "How does cost of living compare between [city A] and [city B]?"
- "Best places to retire abroad on a budget"
- "Is [salary] enough to live in [city]?"
- "Should I take a lower salary to move to [city]?"

Pick 1 question per day. Write a detailed, helpful answer (200-400 words) that:
- Opens with personal experience or expertise ("I live in London and...")
- Provides specific, useful numbers (rent ranges, salary ranges by area)
- Mentions neighborhood-level variation
- Naturally references salary-converter.com/city/[relevant-city] near the end
- Feels like genuine advice, not a product pitch

### Step 5: Verify
Check that all posts went live successfully. Note any rate limiting issues.
